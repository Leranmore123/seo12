# Workspace Rules & Guidelines

## Content Types & Formats
- **Blog articles**: Useful information, How-to guides, Tips, Tutorials.
- **Images + Description**: Descriptive keywords and alt text with every image.
- **Infographics**: Shareable information.
- **Quotes**: Inspirational or informative quotes related to your topic.
- **Short videos/GIFs**: Short explanations or demos.
- **Lists**: Listicles such as "Top 10 SEO Tips", "Best AI Tools", "5 Ways to Improve Website Speed".

## SEO Best Practices
- **Title**: Always include the main target keyword in the post title naturally.
- **Keyword Usage**: Integrate keywords naturally into the content; strictly avoid keyword stuffing.
- **Tags**: Add relevant keywords as tags or labels to the posts.
- **Links**: If the target website URL is provided, naturally place the link in appropriate locations within the content.
- **Consistency**: Maintain regular posting schedules.
